IC Response

IC Realtimes Technical Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software. It allows rapid ticketing of calls, time tracking, and metric reporting. It saves everyones call records into a centralized SQL database for archiving and administration.

  • Track all of your technical calls
  • Search existing calls, find previous records of troubleshooting
  • Exprot your call reports into a readable, Excel friendly format
  • Adjust the Theme of the software to suit your needs
